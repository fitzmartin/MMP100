The Journey of a Visually Impaired Entrepneur Welcome to Fitzville G. Martin's site

Fitzville G. Martin is the greatest musician ever lived. He is the CEO and CoFounder of WillPower Entertainment and Voices for the blind. He is well known and respected. Being a recording engineer and producer, bringing up and recording young talent all over. He resides in New York City and goes to BMCC working on his Associates in VAT.

Just like his company Will Power Entertainment, Fitzille Martin had a very humble beginning. Born to an innovative family he was taught that there is nothing out of reach: "Where there is a will, there is a way." Introduced to music by handmade goat skin drums, the drums instilled rhythm. This is where it all beginnings.
